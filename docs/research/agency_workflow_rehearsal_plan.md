# Agency Workflow Rehearsal Plan

Date: 2026-07-05
Owner: Customer Discovery / GTM Lead
Status: local/offline rehearsal plan

## Objective

Test whether agency operators understand and value the offline launch workspace before building toward external integration.

## Participants

Recruit three operators from these roles:

- Media buyer responsible for weekly ad launches.
- Creative ops manager responsible for asset and naming readiness.
- Account manager or approver responsible for client-safe review.

## Demo Surface

Use only synthetic artifacts:

- `fixtures/fake_agency_creatives/manifest_v2.csv`
- `runs/fake_agency_creatives_v2/workspace.html`
- `runs/fake_agency_creatives_v2/review_packet.md`
- `runs/fake_agency_creatives_v2/launch_plan.json`

## Interview Script

1. How do you launch a batch like this today?
2. Where does the current process waste time?
3. Where do mistakes usually happen?
4. What do you need to see before trusting a batch?
5. Which rows or issues in this demo are confusing?
6. Who would own each fix in your agency?
7. Would this replace a spreadsheet, Ads Manager work, or a review meeting?
8. What would need to change before you would use it weekly?
9. Who would buy it, and what budget would it come from?
10. Would safe export without direct external publishing still be useful?

## Evidence Capture

For every rehearsal, record:

- Participant role.
- Current weekly creative volume.
- Current tool stack.
- Time spent preparing a typical batch.
- Top three workflow pains.
- Confusing product moments.
- Must-have missing fields or previews.
- Buyer role and pricing signal.
- Confidence score from 1 to 5.
- Whether safe export alone is valuable.

## Upgrade/Downgrade Rules

Upgrade product-worthiness if at least two of three operators say the workflow would materially reduce launch-prep or QA time.

Downgrade or pivot if two of three say safe export is not useful without direct external publishing, or if existing tools already solve their review and QA needs.

## Output Artifact

After three rehearsals, create a findings note with:

- What changed in the PRD.
- What changed in the app architecture.
- What changed in GTM positioning.
- Which missing gates remain.
- Whether the project score changes.
